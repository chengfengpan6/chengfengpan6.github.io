I am a Ph.D. graduate from the Department of Information Systems Management at Trinity Business School, Trinity College Dublin, supervised by [Prof. Nicholas P. Danks](https://www.tcd.ie/business/people/faculty-professors/danksn/) and [Prof. Ashish Kumar Jha](https://www.tcd.ie/business/research/the-trinity-centre-for-digital-business--analytics/our-team/akjha/).

My research interests include **information systems**, **business analytics**, and **AI-driven digital strategy**. My current work focuses on adaptive management capability, particularly how organisations use digital technologies, data, and analytics to navigate uncertainty, support decision-making, and drive innovation.
